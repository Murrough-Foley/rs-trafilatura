2018 Boys State Swim Results

Aidan Gantenbein 100 backstroke
Miguel Bernal North Diving
Ben Patton Trinity
Posted: Fri 6:45 PM, Feb 16, 2018  | 
Updated: Sat 8:31 PM, Feb 17, 2018

CLICK FOR FULL STATE SWIM RESULTS

Summary: 5-1A: Salina South diver Keetan Munsell (junior) won the state championship in the event with a score of 409.30.

Trinity Academy junior Ben Patton added to his collection of state titles, winning the 200 yard Individual Medley state title.

In the 100 yard backstroke, Patton was beaten out by Wichita Heights' Aidan Gantenbein for first place at state.

6A: Wichita North's Miguel Bernal took home first in diving for the Redskins.

Wichita West senior David Garcia finished second in the 50 yard freestyle.

Wichita East's Hugh McPherson won first place in the 100 yard freestyle.
